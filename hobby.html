Hobby

Going to concerts is more than a hobby for me; it's a passion. As I enter the arena and feel the energy in the air, my excitement grows. Concerts are more than events for me; they're memories that demonstrate the power of music to bring people together. My first concert back in 2018 was "Voicenotes World Tour." Whenever I have free time, I probably go to concerts.

Playing tennis is one of my hobbies. It is also a source of joy and an active escape. The satisfying thud of the ball against the racket, the swift back-and-forth across the court, and the exhilaration of a well-placed shot all contribute to the unique thrill of the game. I started learning to play tennis when I was 10 years old, ever since I usually spend my weekends evening at the tennis court.

I also like to listen to music as a hobby. Music is my everlasting escape. Music is my ultimate getaway, providing an oasis of relaxation from the stresses of daily life, whether I'm listening to it through earbuds during breaks between classes or creating a playlist for concentrated study sessions.
